About

The Crew Nest at Windcrest is centrally located midway between Ft Sam Houston and Randolph AFB. It is less than 20 minutes to the Riverwalk and downtown amenities!

Ideal for mid-term rental needs of pipeline students and travel nurses, your room will come fully furnished, and has a separate keypad for privacy. In addition to the standard living, dining, and bedrooms there is an office to offer additional space and quiet for studying, and a loft upstairs perfect for game night!
