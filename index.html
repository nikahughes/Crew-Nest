There is a reservation fee equal to $500 due within 72 hours of securing your room. This is not a security deposit; it is applied toward your stay.

Guests in TDY status are not charged a security deposit, however, their card information is stored for any damages beyond normal wear and tear assessed at checkout.

For any changes or cancellations made less than 14 days in advance, you will only be refunded 50% of your reservation fee.

Any notice less than 14 days forfeits the reservation fee.

We understand that sometimes plans change and you may end up leaving earlier than expected. In the event you need to check out sooner than expected, we request the same 14 day notice to check-out without incurring any additional costs. If we are given less than 72 hour notice of an early departure, you will be responsible for full room charges for 72 hours.
